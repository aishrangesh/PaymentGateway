About

COVID-19 Relief Fund was established entirely with public contributions and does not get any budgetary support. We accept voluntary contributions from individuals, organizations, trusts, companies, institutions, etc.All contributions towards the relief fund are exempt from Income Tax under section 80(G).

Vision

A nation in which everyone can get the healthcare they need.

Mission

COVID-19 Relief Fund is a nonprofit organization that works to assist people across the country by offering humanitarian services such as donation, COVID related healthcare facilities,vaccine allocation, etc. We are dedicated to alleviating suffering and disruption caused to families regardless of race, color, religion, or cultural background.

Financial Assistance

Our financial assistance will help your family or someone you know in fighting this deadly disease.
